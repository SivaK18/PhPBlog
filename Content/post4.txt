L&T bags airport construction job worth over Rs 7,000 crore

Larsen & Toubro has won an order worth over Rs 7,000 crore for design and construction of an airport, the engineering major said Monday. The company only said that it has bagged a “major” order for a “major” airport, without disclosing more details.
